About Graybill's Records

Graybill's Records is the go-to store for all of your music needs! Serving central Pennsylvania since 2004, our store has a variety of new and vintage records. We sell and buy records, as well as repair record players of all brands.



Genres

We buy and sell records of all genres! We are currently looking to increase our "pre-listened" stock in the genres of:

  • 90's Rap
  • 70's, 80's, 00's Rock
  • House
  • Punk
  • And other "deep cuts" or rare finds!
